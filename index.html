Qandeel Sajid

Hello! I am a recently graduated from the University of Southern California (USC) with my Masters in Computer Science. During my time at USC, I worked as a graduate research assistent in the Interaction Lab under the advisement of Maja Mataric. As a part of the lab I researched in the field of Human-Robot Interaction.

My primary research interest is in development of robot personalities for the purpose of empowering users with the ability to predict robot behavior given the personality they perceive from the robot. I am currently supported by the National Physical Science Consortium (NPSC) fellowship.
